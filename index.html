Student Groups

What are student groups?


  • Groups of students can be created to track information that relates to that group from attendance, degrees, to health and wellness.
  • Both teachers and administrators can create groups.
  • For instance, one teacher created a group for struggling readers. He implemented a new strategy and then tracked that group over time to see if the strategy positively affected those students.
  • Once you created a group, you can easily view the students’ data, compare the data with other groups, and even share the information with other teachers or admins. This sharing function can also help staff members working in PLCs to track students more efficiently.

Creating a Student Group - Method One


  • We are going to create a new group.
  • Remind users that it can take a few minutes for the group to be available in the dashboard
  • Make sure that your name for the group is informative so you can remember which students are included in the future.
  • Use the description field to provide an explanation for the reason for this group.
  • Private means that only you can use/see the group.
  • Public means the group is available to everyone
  • You don't need to do anything for group origin.
  • Also need to remember to tell participants that they can copy/paste the list of students to add multiple students at once.
  1. Select the tools drop-down menu
  2. Select manage groups
  3. Click on New Group
  4. Fill Out the fields
  5. Be sure to select student from the type dropdown menu
  6. Add students to the group
    • You can add students one at a time or using a list of student IDs.
    • When adding one at a time, start typing in the search field to find the student.
    • After finding the student, select them from the list and you will see their name appear below.
    • If you want to add multiple students all at once, you need to copy/paste their student IDs into the text box.
  7. Click Save when you are finished.
  8. The group should now show up in the group management list

Creating a Student Group - Method Two


  • Let’s make a group of students who scored between the 20th and the 29th percentile on the MAP Math test.
  • First, Click on Assessments, click MAP, then click on Status - Math
  • Click on the bar for the 20th and the 29th percentile

Managing a Student Group


  • Delete the group by clicking on the trash can.
  • You can edit/modify a group by clicking on the pencil icon
  • Remove existing students by clicking on the green checkmark next to their name.
  • You can add them back by click on the red symbol that appears afterwards
  • You can also add students the way you previously learned.
  • Click on the < looking symbol to share the group
  • Click share with user in order to share with one or more users
  • Here are the permissions:
    • None = Not shared with this user
    • Read-Only = Users can view/use the group in the dashboard to filter content, but cannot make changes to the group
    • Collaborate = Users get Read-Only permission and are allowed to add/remove students from the group
  • You get a success message to show you that your changes were applied
  • The visibility column will also update from Private to Shared with Others
